Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Paris, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The Object of the game is to turn over pairs of the matching cards.
